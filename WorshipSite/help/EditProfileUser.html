Edit Profile
   
To change any details stored in your profile, click on the "Edit Profile" link on the left side of the home page. The following information will be displayed.

First Name - Must be entered with no more than 50 characters
Last Name - Must be entered with no more than 50 characters
User Name - Must be entered with at least 4 characters and no more than 50 characters
Password - Only enter a value if you are attempting to change the password. The value must be at least 5 characters and no more than 50 characters
Confirm - This must be entered if a value was entered for password. It is used to verify that the member entered the correct value for Password since the entry is hidden with asterisks (*).
Email 1 - Communication within this system relies on email. To receive information from the worship leader and other team members, you should enter an email address that you check regularly. The value must be entered with no more than 100 characters
Email 2 - If you have a second email (ie. home and work) that you would like to receive emails from the worship leader and other team members, you may enter an email address that you check regularly. The value must be entered with no more than 100 characters
Phone Numbers - At times the worship leader or other team members may need to talk to you directly. Please enter your home/work/cell numbers.
Roles - On the right hand side of the screen is a list of all the roles specified for your team. Please check off all roles that you serve in for your church.

Click on the "Save" button to save all changes or "Cancel" button if you do not want your changes saved.
