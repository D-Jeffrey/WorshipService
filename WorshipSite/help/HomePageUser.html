Home Page
   

This is the initial page displayed after logging in to the TeamWorship website. This screen will display a summary of your future service schedule (My Schedule ) and links to the features you have access to (Quick Links).
The center portion of the screen will contain messages from the site administrator and/or worship leader.
The right site of the screen has a section (Change Requests) which will display existing service change requests. Change requests allow members to indicate that they are not available for a scheduled service date and are looking for someone to take their place.
