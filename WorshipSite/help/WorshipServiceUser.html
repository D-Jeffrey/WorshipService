Worship Service
   
The worship service screen will display the following information for a selected service:

General information
Service Description
Service Contact: This is the name of the individual listed as the primary contact for worship related issues.
Service Date/Time:
Service Notes:
Practice Date/Time:
Practice Notes:

Worship Team
A list of all members assigned to this service is displayed with the following information:
Role: An image and text description of a service role
Member Name: If you hover over the member name, their contact information will be displayed. Clicking on the name will open a mail message in your mail client (eg. Outlook, Outlook Express...).
Equipment Information (optional): If there is equipment required for the role (ie. Microphone type, sound channel), it may display after the member name.

Worship Order
The details of the worship order are displayed with the following information:
Songs:
Key : This is the key that the song will be performed in during the worship service
Song Name: If the song name text is blue, you may click on it to go to the Song Details screen to view the song lyrics.
Video Link Icon : If this icon appears to the right of a song, click on it to view a music video of the song. (please note that we do not host these videos on this site)
Text :
Description : If the description text is blue, you may click on it to see further details.

Resources
Files specific to the service, such as lead sheets or information documents will be displayed. Click on the text to download the file.
