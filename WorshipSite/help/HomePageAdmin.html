Home Page
   
This is the initial page displayed after logging in to the TeamWorship website. This screen will display a summary of your future service schedule (My Schedule ) and links to the features you have access to (Quick Links) as well as links to administration functions (Admin Links).
The center portion of the screen will contain messages from the site administrator and/or worship leader and can be updated through the Edit Home Page link.

The right site of the screen has a section (Change Requests) which will display existing service change requests. Change requests allow members to indicate that they are not available for a scheduled service date and are looking for someone to take their place.
