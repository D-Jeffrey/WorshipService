Worship Calendar
   
The worship calendar will display the current month by default. All scheduled services will be displayed. Services are displayed in blue while associated practices are displayed in red .

Click on the service or practice to display the Worship Service screen with the details of that service.

If there are songs defined in the worship order for a service, the icon will be displayed. If you click on this a PDF file will be downloaded containing a formatted songbook of the songs for that service.

There is a link for the previous and the next months near the top of the screen which will allow navigation from month to month.
